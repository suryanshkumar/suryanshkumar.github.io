Résumé (updated April 2018)

I am a Ph.D. student at the Australian National University Computer Vision Lab, where I am supervised by Yuchao Dai. I also actively work with Hongdong Li and recently collaborated with Anoop Cherian. My research focuses on 3D reconstruction of the scene from images for general dynamic scene and deformable objects. The approaches we proposed are now considered as the benchmark in 3D reconstruction of deformable objects.
I received M.S in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT-Hyderabad, where I also worked as a research assistant in the Robotics Research Center. In the past, I visited e-Motion Group, INRIA Grenoble as a research intern and contributed to autonomous driving project.

Research Interests

Geometric Computer Vision, Mathematical Optimization, Abstract Algebra and Machine Learning (Deep Neural Networks). From time to time, I also investigate topics such as graph theory, topological manifolds and compressed sensing.
