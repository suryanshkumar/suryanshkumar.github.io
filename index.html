    About Me

    I am a PhD student in the research school of Computer Science at Australian National University. Presently, I research on the topic "Non-rigid Structure from Motion" under the guidance of Dr. Yuchao Dai (Supervisor), Prof. Hongdong Li (Chair of Panel) and Prof. Richard Hartley (Supervisor). My research interests are computer vision, machine learning and mathematical optimization. In the past, I worked on robot vision under the supervision Prof. K Madhava Krishna in Robotics Research Centre at IIIT-Hyderabad, India. Besides this, I have also worked as an engineer in Image Understanding Group at Uurmi Systems, Hyderbad.
